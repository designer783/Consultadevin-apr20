# Brand Guidelines - Consulta VIN

## Brand Mission
To provide the most accurate, transparent, and accessible vehicle history data in Mexico, empowering buyers to make confident and safe decisions.

---

## Typography
Our typography system is designed for high readability and a modern, tech-forward aesthetic.

### Display Font: Bricolage Grotesque
**Usage:** Headings (h1 - h6), Hero sections, and primary accents.
**Characteristics:** High-impact, unique character shapes, optimized for large sizes.
**CSS Variable:** `--font-display`
**Tailwind Class:** `font-display`

### Body Font: Rubik
**Usage:** Paragraphs, labels, buttons, and all functional text.
**Characteristics:** Clean, friendly, and highly legible across all digital devices.
**CSS Variable:** `--font-body`
**Tailwind Class:** `font-body`

---

## Color Palette

### Core Brand Colors
*   **Primary Red (`#A51C31`):** Represents energy, urgency, and action. Used for primary CTA buttons and critical indicators.
*   **Brand Navy (`#2C234D`):** Represents trust, stability, and professional authority. Used for navigation and deep text.
*   **Accent Orange (`#F9B248`):** Represents vibrancy and approachability. Used for highlights and supportive visual elements.

### Secondary Colors
*   **Brand Light (`#F5F1FE`):** Clean background color for sections and cards.
*   **Dark Gray (`#4A4A4A`):** Used for secondary text to provide hierarchy.

---

## Visual Language

### Spacing & Layout
*   Section Padding: Standardized at `py-20` (80px) for consistent rhythm.
*   Border Radius: Default at `0.75rem` (12px) for a soft yet professional look.
*   Borders: Subtle `1px` borders using `--border` color.

### Grid System
*   12-column grid for complex layouts.
*   Standardized gutters of `gap-8` to `gap-12`.

---

## Component Guidelines
*   **Buttons:** Always use the `Button` component with defined variants. Avoid custom inline styles.
*   **Cards:** Use `shadow-md` for standard cards and `shadow-2xl` for highlighted "Hero" cards.
*   **Icons:** Use Lucide React icons. Stroke width should be consistent at `2px`.
